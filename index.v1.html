Common questions

Everything schools usually ask.

How long does it take to go live? +
14 days from contract signing. Days 1-3 we set up your school's space and import your classes, sections, and fee structure. Days 4-7 we move your student and staff records from your existing system. Days 8-11 we train your admin, principal, teachers, and parents. Days 12-14 you go live with one class, then roll out across the school. We handle the data migration end-to-end.
We already use [School ERP X]. Can you migrate our data? +
Yes. Our team takes data exports from common school software (Entab, Educomp, Fedena, custom systems, even Excel sheets). We match the fields to AIvenX, check for errors, and confirm everything with you before importing. You don't reformat anything — that's our job.
Where is our data stored? Is it safe? +
All data is hosted in India and follows the country's data protection rules. Each school's data stays completely separate — no other school ever sees yours. Daily backups with the ability to restore to any point in time. You can export everything in one click, anytime. For face attendance, we store secure math signatures of faces, never the photos themselves. Your students' data is never used to train any outside AI.
What happens if we don't like AIvenX after the pilot? +
The 14-day pilot is risk-free. If you walk away, you take a full data export with you — no termination fee, no contract trap. We'd rather you join honestly than be locked in unhappily.
Does AI replace our teachers? +
No — and we'd refuse to build that. AI generates draft chapters, draft questions, draft grades. A teacher reviews and approves before any student sees the output. Cortex saves time on prep and grading; teaching stays human.
Do I need a fingerprint scanner for face attendance? +
No special equipment. Any USB or laptop webcam works. A tablet at the classroom door is enough for a class of 40. If a student's face isn't recognised on a particular day (low light, sudden hairstyle change), an ID-card scan works as a fallback.
What about pricing? +
Pricing scales with student count and edition — Starter (Operate), Standard (Operate + Cortex), or AI-Native (Everything). After a demo, we send a tailored quote within 24 hours. No public pricing sheet because no two schools have the same needs — and we'd rather have one honest conversation than print a number that doesn't fit.
Which boards / curricula do you support? +
IB (PYP / MYP / DP), CBSE, ICSE / ISC, Cambridge (CAIE / IGCSE / A-Levels), Advanced Placement, all major State Boards, and NIOS — ready from day one, with each board's own report card format. If you follow a different curriculum, share your grading scheme and we'll set it up during onboarding.
