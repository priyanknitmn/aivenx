Every AI output is reviewed and approved by a teacher before a student sees it. The teacher gains hours; never gets replaced.

Same chapter. Four ways to learn it.

Cortex generates the same syllabus in four persona-shaped modes. Students take a quick psychometric quiz; we recommend the right mode for them — and they can switch any time.

Smart

Clean, exam-aligned, structured. For students who want the syllabus distilled to its essentials.

SPARK

Fun

Playful, real-world, why-it-matters. Great for visual learners and curious minds.

QUEST

Game

Goal-driven, gamified, level-up. XP, streaks, quick-checks per topic.

SAGA

Story

Narrative, character-led. Concepts taught through story arcs.

Generation time: ~30 seconds per chapter. Teacher reviews, edits, and approves before students see it. Boards covered: CBSE · ICSE · IB · State boards · Grades 1–12.

03 · Guardian 22 / 30
Guardian

Eyes on every gate, every bus, every child.

📷

Face-AI campus gate

Real-time recognition at school entry. <100ms / frame. Auto IN/OUT events. Visitor sign-in flow. Parent gets a notification when their child enters or leaves campus.

🚌

Live bus tracking

GPS-fed map. ETA, route status, current stop, driver name, occupancy. "Bus arriving in 12 min" pings to the parent's phone before pickup time.

🔊

In-bus aggression detection BETA

Audio + vision AI on monitored routes. Detects raised voices, isolation, exclusion. Alerts the bus monitor + school admin when threshold crossed.

📋

Visitor audit log

Sign-in form: name · ID · purpose · host. 30-day retention. CSV/PDF export for compliance audits. Auto-flag on unknown faces or repeat visits.

Every Guardian feature is independently toggleable per tenant. DPDP-Act consent flows for face-AI; schools that prefer can use RFID-card attendance instead.
