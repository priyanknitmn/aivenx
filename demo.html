Feature 01 · Admissions

Capture every enquiry. Convert more of them.

A full enquiry-to-enrolment funnel. Every walk-in, web form, and phone call lands in one queue with follow-up reminders.

What it does

  • • Online enquiry form embedded on your school website
  • • Walk-in capture by the front desk in 30 seconds
  • • Stage-tracked pipeline: Enquired → Visited → Tested → Offered → Enrolled
  • • Auto-reminders for follow-up calls, missed steps
  • • Document uploads (birth cert, previous report card, photo) — all in one record
  • • Direct conversion to a student record on enrolment — no re-typing

Why it matters

Most schools lose 30% of enquiries between the form and the first follow-up call. AIvenX prevents that by making every enquiry visible to admin, principal, and the front desk on day one.

Feature 08 · Assessment Creator

Build a 3-hour board-style paper in 4 minutes.

Pick filters → preview coverage → publish. CBSE-standard section presets included. Live distribution chart shows you exactly what students will face.

How it works

  1. 1. Pick subject, grade, total marks, duration.
  2. 2. Choose section structure — or load a preset (CBSE Sec A/B/C/D/E).
  3. 3. Per section, set: question type, count, marks each, chapter & difficulty filters.
  4. 4. Live preview shows: chapter coverage %, Bloom distribution, expected difficulty curve.
  5. 5. Auto-fill from question bank, or hand-pick. Mix freely.
  6. 6. Publish → printable PDF + answer key + student-portal link.

Why teachers love it

Paper-setting used to take 4-6 hours. With Cortex it's under 10 minutes — and the coverage preview catches mistakes before they reach students. Two teachers can collaborate live on the same paper.
