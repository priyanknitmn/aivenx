Schools are drowning in tools.
Teachers, in tabs.

The average Indian K-12 school logs into 5–7 separate platforms every day. Teachers copy-paste between them. Parents miss messages on WhatsApp. AI is sprinkled on top — never in the spine.

🧩
Vendor sprawl

Six vendors. One school.

ERP for fees, LMS for content, separate CCTV for safety, WhatsApp for parents, three Excel sheets for HR. Nothing talks. Renewals every quarter.

📑
Teacher burnout

4 hours of paperwork. 2 hours of teaching.

Lesson plans typed by hand every Sunday. Worksheets scrambled the morning of. 40 answer scripts graded after school every week.

📵
Parent fatigue

37 messages in the WhatsApp group.

Fee reminder, picnic permission, homework photo, sports kit notice — buried under "Good morning ji" stickers. The one that mattered? Missed.

🚸
Safety blind spots

Did my child reach school?

Bus tracker on one app. Gate logbook in a Reception register. Bullying never escalates beyond the class teacher's diary.

Marks aren't insight

"He scored 72%." Now what?

No view of where he's stuck. No comparison to class median. No prescribed practice. Next term starts the same way.

🤖
AI as a sticker

"AI-powered" everything. AI-native nothing.

A chatbot bolted on. A summarizer in settings. The actual product still does what it did in 2018. Schools deserve better.

08 · FAQ

Questions, asked honestly.

Does the evaluator really read handwritten Indian-English?

Yes. Trained on Indian school answer scripts (CBSE / ICSE / state boards), grade 5 onward. Confidence shown per question — anything below threshold is flagged for teacher review, never silently auto-graded.

How long does onboarding take?

Two days for a new school with clean rosters, two weeks if migrating from another ERP. We bring our team for the first migration; you don't lift a CSV.

Is the AI replacing my teachers?

No. AI removes drafting and grading drudgery. Every AI-generated artifact lands in front of a teacher who edits and approves before students see it. Your teachers gain 6–8 hours a week.

Where is student data stored?

In Indian data centres. Encrypted in transit and at rest. DPDP Act 2023 minor-consent flows and right-to-erasure baked in.

Can we use only the AI Studio?

Yes. Cortex is available standalone for schools that already run an ERP. Plug in via SSO + a roster sync, and your teachers get the studio.

What about face-recognition consent?

Per-student consent collected before enrollment. Audit log shows who collected when. Schools can opt for RFID/ID-card attendance instead — every Guardian feature is independently toggleable per tenant.
