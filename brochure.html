AIvenX · Cortex AI Studio Page 4

Attendance, reinvented

Face AI replaces
the biometric machine.

A single webcam at the classroom door — or a tablet at the gate — marks every student present in under a second. No fingerprint queues, no proxy attendance, no missed entries.

How it works

  1. 1. Enrol once. Capture 3-5 photos of each student during admission. Embeddings are stored, photos are not.
  2. 2. Recognise live. The classroom camera streams frames. Cortex matches faces in real time and writes attendance to the gradebook.
  3. 3. Default-absent policy. Anyone not seen by the cut-off is automatically marked absent. Parents get an SMS.
  4. 4. Period-wise tracking. Same logic for every period — absconding mid-day is detected too.

Why it matters

  • ✓Zero teacher time spent on roll-call
  • ✓Proxy-proof — no impersonation
  • ✓Hardware-free — uses any webcam
  • ✓Privacy-first — only embeddings stored
  • ✓Works offline-first; syncs when online

Real-world impact

A 1,000-student school saves ~250 staff-hours per term on attendance alone — and gets fully audited records in return.
